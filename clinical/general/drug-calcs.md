## Basic

### Paediatric weight

$$ \textit{Weight} = (\text{Age} + 4) * 2 $$

### Volume required

Key here is to ensure everything in same unit (so all mg or all g)

$$ \textit{Volume required} = \frac{\text{Strength required}} {\text{Stock strength}} + \frac {\text{Volume of stock}} 1 $$
