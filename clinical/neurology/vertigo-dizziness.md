Vertigo ranges from benign to life threatning (e.g. CVA or cardiovascular cause)

## :triangular_flag_on_post: Red Flags
1. Neurological signs or symptoms
2. Acute deafness
3. New type/rapid onset headache
4. Vertical nystagmus
