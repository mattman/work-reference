## Cardiogenic

- Left heart failure
  - Arrythmia
- Acute Myocardial Infarction
- Peri/endo carditis

## Non-Cardiogenic
- Burns
- Pancreatitis
- Disseminated Intravascular Coagulopathy
- Immersion
- Septicaemia
- Head Injury
- Pulmonary Embolism
- Eclampsia

- Increased vascular volume
  - Fluid overload
    - Non-compliance with dialysis/diuretics/fluid restriction
  - Renal failure
  
- Baroceptors and therefore RAAS causes some problems
